Welcome to BajaBronco.com, your top source for information on the Stroppe Baja Broncos and CIBIE lighting products. We've just got back from the 2013 NORRA Mexican 1000! Together with Kaysinger Racing we came 3rd in our class and 21st overall for the Vintage Class. Our Stroppe Racing Bronco, the 1969 Baja 1000-winner, was used in the upcoming film "Need For Speed", but is now back in our shop for a period restoration. Our frame-up restoration of the 4-speed 1972 Baja Broncois done. We also just finished up this '73 Baja for another Customer - check the restoration section for more info. Some new Broncos For Sale are on their way here too! Need some read? -check out The "Firestone" Baja or The Travel Trailer Baja.
