It's been in covered storage since then. It has a salvage title which is a bummer, but it's all original and everything is there and I can't find any accident damage. Not sure how it got the branded title. I've been looking for a 62-3 Meteor for a while and finally found one in my area and for sale - I'm stoked. Email Andrew
