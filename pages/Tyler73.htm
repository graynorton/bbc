Tyler's '73 Stroppe Baja Bronco

Tyler's Baja has been in the family since his grandfather ordered it in early '73. It came highly optioned from Stroppe and the dealer with a Warn winch bumper and 8000 lb. winch complete with in-cab controls, Cibie lights, Clardy air conditioning, dual exhaust with glasspacks, and rear swaybar. On top of all those original options, Tyler has updated the Baja with some modifications.

To start with he moved the Cibie lights around to the rear bumper and added some high power aftermarket lights to the original winch bumper. Tyler had upgraded the stock suspension with a Wild Horses 2.5" when I took these photos at the Bronco 30th anniversary. Other mods include a front cage kit for the rollbar and an Autometer Tachometer. Rounding out the Baja is a Kayline Convertible top over the original paint job.
