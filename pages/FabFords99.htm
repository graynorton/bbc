Baja Broncos at Fabulous Fords Forever

Join fellow Ford enthusiasts at the 14th annual Fabulous Fords Forever car show at Knott's Berry Farm in Anaheim, CA, April 18th, 1999. This is one of the biggest all-Ford shows in the country. There are always a couple of rows full of Early Broncos. Last year there were around 70, including 4 Baja Broncos and one Stroppe racer! These pictures are from last year's show.

Information and Registration

To register for Fabulous Fords Forever, visit the Ford Car Clubs website at www.fordcarclubs.org. They have a registration form that you can download, print and send in. Hope to see you there! If anyone would like to caravan to the event from Northern California with us, please email me.
