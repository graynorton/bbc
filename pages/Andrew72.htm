Andrew's '72 Stroppe Baja Bronco

We Just got another Baja Bronco after selling our 73 last week. This one is a '72 with Stroppe-installed power steering and automatic. The Baja has been owned by the same family since '73 and is very original throughout.  This truck was very well taken care of and sometimes saw duty as a hunting rig.  We are going to put a set of Stroppe wheels on it and see about getting it back to factory colors. Ultimately, we'd like to do a body-off restoration.

Some of you may be wondering what's up with the paint job; so are we! The previous owner who bought it in '73 said it had the orange top and white hood when he got it. Upon closer inspection, the paint appears to be Ford factory original on the lower body. Only the top, hood, and tops of the fenders have been painted. Perhaps the original owner decided 4 colors was 2 too many.
