Fabulous Fords Forever — 1999

We're back from the Fabulous Fords Forever show in Buena Park, CA and we had a great time. We had excellent weather and there were all kinds of Fords out on display. Four Stroppes, and a total of 86 Early Broncos showed up. Check out the pictures!
